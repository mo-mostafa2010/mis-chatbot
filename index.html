ABOUT US

INSPIRED BY SUSTAINABILITY ... POWERING MOBILITY

MOBILITY INTEGRATED SOLUTIONS
(MIS)

MIS is a private limited liability company based in Egypt established in 2018. We focuses on technology and smart mobility solutions, software platforms, ICT, smart cities and sustainable development.

MIS was created with the aim to transform the transportation and ICT sector to a more reliable form, accommodating the state-of-the-art transportation technologies, shared-mobility solutions and latest ICT technological innovations.

OUR VISION

Provide mobility and ICT solutions for smarter and more efficient sustainable cities. Revolutionize the transportation, Smart Cities and IT infrastructure in Egypt and the MENA region.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to serve as a ‘implementers’ for new revolutionary ideas related to the transportation and ICT sectors and smarter cities that have true economic potential, benefits and value to all stakeholders. Through our connections with the industry and the academia, we are keen on enhancing and optimization of already existing systems and facilities through scientifically analyzing challenges and providing suitable solution mechanisms. Through our expertise, we also serve as ‘advisors’ for investors and governmental entities wishing to utilize innovative solutions, whether products or services.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • Optimized solutions that fit into clients needs through technology initiatives
  • All the alternative approaches that lower costs and improve delivery of our services
  • Work closely with its partners by providing them with continuous support, trustworthy relationships, valuable connections and resources
